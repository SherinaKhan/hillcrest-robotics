Vex Competition

What is the Vex Competition?

An annual worldwide competition for robotics where teams compete from all over New York. Students from elementary school through univeristy are eligible to compete. The game changes every year. They set the requirements for robots.

Robotics Team Impact in Vex

Each year Hillcrest robotics team builds the robots meeting the requirements for the competition. A few game that our robotics team have participated in the past was making the robot pick up items and try to get them in the hoop first before other teams could finish before them
