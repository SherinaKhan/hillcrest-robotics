Teams

There are two different squads in the robotics team. There is the mechanical squad and the coding squad, who each serve different purposes. Both of these squads work together to design and create the robots.

Mechanical Squad

The mechanical squad's main objective is creating the physical appearance of the robot. Not only do they focus on the robot's physical design but, they also manage the robotic circuitry.

The Mechanical Squad's Building Process

1) The mechanical and coding squads will each their own create plan for the robot's design. Then, they will vote on which idea is better.

2) Engineering notebook (needed for documenting significant details during the engineering process and is an utlizied as an ongoing record of a project)

3) Design structure of the robot

4) Get the code from the coding squad and upload it into the robot.

5) Building test models to see if the structure will work

6) Building the final robot

Coding Squad

The coding squad basically put the life in dead robotic structures. They use their knowledge of computer science to program the robots. Their impact with programming controls these structures on how the robots interpret the world, as well as their movements and actions.

The Coding Squad's Building Process

1) Design a circuit diagram to see tract the devices and ports

2) Use the VEX Cortex as microprocessor board to program the robots.

3) Make an algorithm and flowchart for the robot.

4) The algorithms are tested on test bots using a test bed.

5) During the testing process it is essential to use the test bed to ensure that either, the motor is moving or wheels are spinning.

6) When the main structure is ready, the coding is uploaded.
